Customizable Food Dispenser

Tech Used

Raspberry pi, FT90 continous rotation servo motors, custom 3d printed dispensing mechanism, python GUIZero, 7-inch touchscreen monitor, digital load cell + HX711, 12V power supply

What we created

A compact, customizable, user-friendly automatic food dispenser that can dispense up to 10 dry food products on demand

Lessons learned

  • Full system design and documentation including writing a needs statement, project goals, objectives, evaluation of alternatives, design constraints & feasibility, system description and design validation
  • Testing a load cell sensor
  • Building and printing custom 3d parts

Ideas to make it better

  • A more accurate weight sensor for calculating how much food was dispensed so users can be accurately charged at a fixed cost per lb.
